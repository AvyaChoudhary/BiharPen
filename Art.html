The Art Of Bihar

Hey guys! Wanna take a ride through the awesome world of Bihar’s art? Buckle up, because this state is packed with super cool and ancient art forms that are totally lit! Let’s dive in and see what makes Bihar’s art scene pop.

Think embroidery is only something for grandparents? Think again! Absolutely cool are the Sujani embroidery from Bihar. Women from there stitch up stories into clothes, making the most marvelous quilts and sarees. Each piece like a short comic strip of yore—there's just so much storytelling in each stitch!

Ever heard of Madhubani painting?It's like classic art style has been going on forever! This art comes from Bihar villages. It's like a super cool vibe; people even use twigs, their fingers, and sometimes even matchsticks to get the stuff done. Amazingly enough, the paints are made of flowers, turmeric, and clay. Well, I guess that would be pretty eco-friendly, too!

We all have tried playing with playdough and clay. Well, potters of Bihar are pros at that. From making simple water pots-matka-to fancy decoration pieces, these artists seriously master the craft. Simple-yet classy designs are definitely Pinterest-worthy!
